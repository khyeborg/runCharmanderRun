Pokemon Game

Description:

Since this is an open-ended game, I decided to have a ton of fun with it and create a Pokemon game. I like the idea of a main Pokemon collecting fairy Pokemons while avoiding Pokemons that are super effective towards it. I downloaded Pokemon sprites and decided on the specific Pokemons I want to use. I wanted for the game to be funny so I introduced 'Ouch' sounds when Charmander collides with water Pokemons. If Charmander collides with water Pokemons for 5 times, it faints and a sentimental instrumental from Pokemon Black and White gets played. If Charmander wins, Pikachu and Togepi get really happy for him!
